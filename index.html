Paddle safe out there

Quick reminders and data sources behind this app.

🛡️ Before you go

•Wear a leash. It keeps you and your board together.
•Wear a PFD (personal flotation device). This is required on Auckland waters when off the beach.
•Tell someone your plan: where, who, and when you'll be back.
•Check wind forecasts as offshore wind can push you out fast.
•Don't paddle alone if you're a beginner.

💧 Water quality

Auckland's Safeswim programme monitors beach water quality and issues alerts after heavy rainfall or sewage overflows. If a beach is flagged unsafe, avoid entering the water — even briefly.

If in doubt, sit it out. Water quality can change within hours.

→ safeswim.org.nz
